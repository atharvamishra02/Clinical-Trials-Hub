# Clinical Trials Hub

A comprehensive intelligence platform designed to provide actionable insights into the clinical trial landscape. Beyond a simple data repository, the **Clinical Trials Hub** transforms raw trial data into meaningful views, summarizations, and strategic intelligence for clinical, medical, and business development teams.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The Clinical Trials Hub is an advanced search and analysis engine that aggregates data from diverse sources including ClinicalTrials.gov, PubMed, OpenTargets, and corporate press releases. It is built to answer complex, high-stakes questions that require deep domain knowledge and context-aware filtering.

### Example Intelligent Queries
- *"Identify all active interventional trials for Pediatric Crohn's disease that are using a 'Head-to-Head' design comparing a JAK inhibitor against an Anti-TNF agent, excluding any trials that use a placebo arm."*
- *"I am working on first-in-class mAb development against OSMRβ in ulcerative colitis, show me all relevant clinical information."*
- *"Show trials targeting EGFR in oncology."*

## 🛠 Core Use Cases

The platform is optimized for the following strategic functions:

| Category | Key Activities |
| :--- | :--- |
| **General Dashboard** | High-level metrics: trials by phase, sponsor distribution, and therapeutic area trends. |
| **Trial Design** | Benchmarking cohort selection, endpoint definitions (primary/secondary), dosage selection, and arm design. |
| **Site & Market Intelligence** | Identifying patient populations, hospitals, and Principal Investigators (PIs) by country. |
| **Strategic Assessment** | TPP (Target Product Profile) definition, Competitive Landscape, and Indication Expansion. |
| **Risk & Pipeline Analysis** | Success rates by target/indication and critical analysis of trial readout press releases. |

## 🧪 Entity & Search Taxonomy

The hub utilizes a multi-level taxonomy to ensure precision in searching and filtering.

### 1. Diseases
- **Category:** e.g., Cardiovascular, Neuroimmune, Oncology
- **Disease:** e.g., Lung Cancer, Crohn's Disease
- **Subcategory/Stage:** e.g., ALK-Positive Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC)

### 2. Drugs & Interventions
- **Modality:** Small molecule, Biologic, mAb, cell therapy, etc.
- **Mechanism of Action (MoA):** Class-based filtering (e.g., Lipid Modifying Agents, JAK inhibitors).
- **Target/Biology:** Specific molecular targets (e.g., EGFR, OSMRβ).
- **Administration:** Route of administration (Oral, IV, SubQ).
- **Naming:** Generic, Brand, and Investigation Code names.

### 3. Sponsors
- **Commercial:** Pharma and Biotech companies.
- **Institutional:** Academic centers, government bodies, and NGOs.

## 📊 Trial Intelligence & Filters

- **Timeline:** Period-based filtering, start dates, and projected completion.
- **Recruitment:** Real-time status tracking and historical recruitment trends.
- **Readouts & Results:** Success/failure classification, primary endpoint achievement, and data analysis.
- **Geographic Data:** Study sites, country-level distribution, and PI identification.

- **Omics & Biology:** OpenTargets, UniProt.
- **Market Intelligence:** Corporate Press Releases, SEC filings.
